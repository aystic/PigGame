RULES OF THE GAME :

  • The game starts with player 1
  • The player which reaches a total score of 100 first wins
  • A player can roll dice any number of times which causes the number on the dice to add up to the "Gamble score" of the player
  • The "Gamble score" is added to the total score on clicking hold and other player becomes the active player
  • In case the dice shows the number "1" then the player loses all the gamble score and loses his turn as well
